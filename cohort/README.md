## How Cohorts Work

### Week 1 (Saturdays)
- 2-hour live session with Sid
- Live lecture (concept) + live demo (Sid building) + Q&A
- Recording posted within 24 hours
- Slack channel for your cohort

### Mon-Fri
- 1 hour/day building on your own
- Daily Slack check-in: ✅ done / 🔴 stuck / 🎯 tomorrow
- Sid + Ship Captains help in Slack
- Office hours on Wednesdays (optional, recorded)

### Demo Day (final Saturday)
- 5-minute demo per builder (live or pre-recorded Loom)
- Builder #N awarded to those who passed all 18 verifications
- Top 10 demos featured by Sid on LinkedIn

## Founding Builder Status (First 100)

The first 100 builders to ever complete OneMillion get:

- **Founding Builder permanent badge** on their public profile
- **Sid's personal Slack DM access** (forever)
- **Personal intro from Sid** to one investor OR one hiring manager (your pick) — within 30 days of graduating

This benefit is closed at 100 builders. If you're reading this in 2027+ and the count is at 87, you can still earn it. If it's at 100+, you've missed it.

2. What's the product idea you want to build?

Sid personally reviews every application. Expect a response within 5 days.

> **Selection isn't about resume.** It's about clarity of motivation. Yoga teachers, retirees, EAs, executives, engineers all get in. What matters: you have a real reason to ship and you'll actually do the work.

## What If I Can't Make Saturday Sessions?

The recording posts within 24 hours. Watching the recording is fine. ~40% of cohort builders are async-only.

## Ship Captains

If you've already graduated (Builder #N exists) and want to help run the next cohort: apply to be a **Ship Captain**. Get paid in OneMillion equity-of-mission (the gratitude tier) + access to Sid's network. Ship Captain details will be added before the first public cohort opens.
