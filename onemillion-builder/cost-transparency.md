# Cost Transparency

**Total realistic cost over 18 days: $5–25.**

You will not be surprised. Here's exactly what you'll pay, where, and why.

---

## The Honest Breakdown

| Item | Cost | When | Required? |
|------|------|------|-----------|
| **Anthropic API credits** | $5–15 | Throughout (more in Weeks 2-3 with AI features) | Yes |
| **Custom domain** (e.g., yourapp.com) | $10–12/year | Day 14 | Optional |
| **Supabase** | $0 | Day 5 onwards | Free tier covers it |
| **Vercel** | $0 | Day 4 onwards | Free tier covers it |

| **Sentry** | $0 | Day 15 | Free tier covers it |
| **UptimeRobot** | $0 | Day 15 | Free tier covers it |

> ⚠️ **Important:** If you already pay $20/month for Claude.ai (the chat product), that does NOT cover API usage. You need the separate API key from console.anthropic.com. Sorry, that's how Anthropic structured it.

---

## Why The Range ($5–25)?

**Lower end ($5):**
- You're efficient with prompts (don't re-run things needlessly)
- You skip the custom domain (stay on `yourapp.vercel.app`)
- You use Claude Haiku or Sonnet (not Opus) for most builds

**Higher end ($25):**
- You re-run agents to iterate on PRDs and code (totally fine)
- You buy a custom domain (~$10-12/year — looks more professional)
- You use Opus for complex builds (more expensive but smarter)

**Average builder spends: $10-15.**

---

## What If Money Is Tight?

I built this to be accessible. If $5–15 is a real barrier:

1. **Anthropic free credits.** They start you with $5. That covers Week 1 entirely.
2. **Skip the custom domain.** Stay on `yourapp.vercel.app` forever. Functionally identical, just less brand-able.
3. **Use Claude Haiku for most builds.** It's a fraction of the cost of Sonnet. The course uses Sonnet by default but Haiku works for most tasks.
4. **Apply for a course scholarship.** If you genuinely can't afford the Anthropic credits, DM Sid on [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/siddharthdixit) — the OneMillion Crew has a small fund to cover API costs for builders who need it. No questions asked, no paperwork.

## What I'm NOT Selling You

To make this clear: the course never asks you to pay anything to me, the OneMillion brand, or any partner I'm affiliated with.

- ❌ No "premium tier" course content
- ❌ No paid certificate (Builder #N is free, forever)
- ❌ No paid Discord / Slack tier
- ❌ No upsold "advanced bootcamp"
- ❌ No affiliate links that mark up prices

The only money you spend goes directly to:
- **Anthropic** for AI compute
- **Optional: your domain registrar** for `yourapp.com`

That's it. Forever.

---

## Why Free?

I wrote about this in [the Manifesto](./MANIFESTO.md). Short version: the skill of building with AI cannot become a class signal. It cannot be gated by who can afford it. It has to be a baseline available to anyone, anywhere, with a laptop and the will to learn.

Free is a hard constraint, not a feature.
