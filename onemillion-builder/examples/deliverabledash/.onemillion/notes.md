# Day 2 Notes

## Target User

Freelance UX designers with 3-8 active clients who send weekly status updates manually.

## Conversation 1 — Maya, freelance UX designer

**Date:** 2026-05-19  
**Problem evidence:** Maya spends 60-90 minutes every Friday checking Notion, email, and Slack before writing client updates.  
**Current workaround:** A Notion table plus manual Gmail drafts.  
**Cost:** Updates are often late, which makes clients ask for status in Slack.

## Conversation 2 — Leo, brand designer

**Date:** 2026-05-19  
**Problem evidence:** Leo forgets small revision tasks after client calls.  
**Current workaround:** Apple Notes and calendar reminders.  
**Cost:** He has missed two low-priority deliverables this quarter.

## Conversation 3 — Priya, product designer

**Date:** 2026-05-20  
**Problem evidence:** Priya writes Sunday-night summaries for five clients and hates starting from a blank page.  
**Current workaround:** Copies last week's email and edits it.  
**Cost:** Sunday admin work eats into family time.

## Decision

Build for freelance designers who need deliverable tracking plus faster weekly update drafts.
