## The Mom Test

- **[Rob Fitzpatrick's 20-min YouTube talk](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=mom+test+rob+fitzpatrick)** — if you don't want to read
- **[The 3 Biggest Mistakes In Customer Discovery](https://www.startups.com/library/expert-advice/3-biggest-mistakes-customer-discovery)** — short article
